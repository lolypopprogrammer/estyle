ABOUT
In the past, after spending too much money on clothes, some of which I rarely wore, I was still unhappy with the way the outfits looked. I could see myself standing in front of a mirror saying: “Something doesn’t feel right, but I can’t put my finger on it.”

For the past 8 years living in Dubai, as a personal stylist, I’ve been on a mission to show people how to dress up, stand out in the crowd and feel self-confident on their way to success.

My name is Lucia Csobonyei and I created estyleshaker for women who feel overwhelmed by their unorganized closets and stressed out when they go shopping.
Every advice and recommendation is based on yearslong trials and errors, styling experience, skills and last but not least my clients’ concerns and requirements.

Their outfits speak for themselves because all my clients are true estyleshakers.

Once you explore your guide, our secret styling formulas will make much more sense. To uncover more, dive into the estyleshaker video channels and become a part of our fashionable family.
